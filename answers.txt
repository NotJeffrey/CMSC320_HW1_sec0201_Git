Q1: Why might we consider using Git?
Answer: Git is an excellent way to collaborate on a project and is a valuable tool for version control.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.
Answer: Data science can be applied to finance to analyze the effects of world events on markets.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?
Answer: Merging keeps history of all branches and shows where the branches were combined while Rebasing moves a branch's commits to the tip of another branch.
